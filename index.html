About PixelLab


Welcome to PixelLab:

PixelLab is a passion project aimed at helping you create pixel art easily and effectively. Designed for both newcomers and experienced artists, it provides a simple, intuitive platform for your creative projects. We hope you find it useful and enjoyable.


Features:

  • Customizable canvas sizes and resolutions
  • Variety of drawing tools and brushes
  • Color palettes
  • Export options

Version Information:

PixelLab Version: 1.0.0

Release Date: October 2024
